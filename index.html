About me

Hi! My name is Bipin Acharya.

Hi! My name is Bipin Acharya. I'm based in Pokhara, where I live with my family. Back in 2021, I was pursuing my dream of becoming a web developer, even though many people aim for professions like medicine or law. Now, I’ve completed my Bachelor of Science (Hons.) in Computing from Informatics College, Pokhara, and am working as a Senior Flutter Developer at Able Innovation, where I’ve been developing apps for over a year.

Throughout my journey, I've always been passionate about modern technology, especially programming, web design, and app development. Besides work, I have a deep love for traveling, experiencing nature, watching movies, and anime. These hobbies inspire my creativity and give me fresh perspectives on the projects I work on.
